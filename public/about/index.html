About Me

Gurudev Ilangovan is a graduate research assistant in the department of computer science and engineering at Texas A&M University. As part of his research and coursework, Gurudev is involved in a lot of data science projects. His research areas include sequential data mining and entity deduplication.

With a skillset that spans statistical thinking, optimization, simulation machine learning, building applications (such as web apps and R packages) and programming, his expertise lies in engineering data driven solutions to real world problems.

Ever so excited to take on new challenges, Gurudev strives to make the world a better place through data science, one problem at a time.
